Welcome to the Global Journalist website.
Committed to sharing the news of the world through the citizens of the world, Global Journalist specializes in acquiring pieces of citizen journalism for their stories. We dive more in-depth about citizen journalism, what it is, and what citizen journalism means to the average American at our About Us page.

What does Global Journalist focus on?

Citizen journalism is increasing in prevalence. When someone records an event via video or writing, that is considered citizen journalism. The video or writing can be used by professional companies (with permission) to post on their website following a story regarding the same event.
